BAHAR CAFE

Bahar Cafe is a F&B-Casual Dining outlet that offers a wide variety of delicious and authentic Indian cuisine. The menu features a variety of dishes from North Indian, South Indian, and Chinese cuisines. The restaurant has a cozy and inviting atmosphere, with comfortable seating and a warm ambiance. The staff is friendly and attentive, and the service is prompt and efficient. The food is freshly prepared and cooked to perfection. The prices are reasonable and the portions are generous. Bahar Cafe is the perfect place to enjoy a delicious meal with family and friends.
